# OruBar Pay — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 12 July 2026

OruBar Pay is built on one principle: **your data never leaves your phone.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** OruBar Pay has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. The app
does not even request the `INTERNET` permission, so the Android operating system
itself prevents it from sending any data off your device. You can verify this by
using the app in airplane mode — it works exactly the same.

## What the app stores on your device

A local payment history, saved only in the app's private storage on your phone:

- the payee's UPI ID (VPA),
- the payee's name (if the QR code contained one),
- the amount,
- the date and time,
- whether the *99# dialer was opened.

This history is **excluded from cloud backup** and is never transmitted anywhere.
You can erase it at any time from the History screen. Uninstalling the app also
deletes it.

## What the app never sees or stores

- Your **UPI PIN**. You enter it inside your bank's secure *99# session, never in
  this app. OruBar Pay has no PIN field of any kind.
- Your bank account number, card details, or balance.
- Your contacts, location, messages, or call logs.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — used only to scan QR codes, on-device. No photos are saved or sent.

That is the only permission the app requests. There is no SMS permission, no phone
permission, and no internet permission.

## How payment actually happens

OruBar Pay reads a UPI QR code and opens your phone's dialer with the NPCI *99#
code. The payment itself runs on your mobile carrier's and bank's secure *99#
(USSD) service. OruBar Pay never handles, holds, or moves money.
