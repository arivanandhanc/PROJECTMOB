## Full description
```
Pay at any shop even with no internet and one bar of signal.

OruBar Pay reads a shop's payment QR code and opens your phone's *99# service so
you can pay on your bank's own secure system — no data pack, no heavy app, works on
old and low-cost phones.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Scan the shop's QR code with your camera.
2. Check the name and amount on a big, clear screen.
3. Tap Pay — your phone dialer opens *99#.
4. Follow the simple steps and enter your UPI PIN in your bank's screen.

BUILT FOR TRUST
• Works fully offline — this app does not even have internet access.
• You enter your PIN in your bank's *99# screen, never in this app.
• Only one permission: Camera (to scan the QR).
• Your payment history stays on your phone only. Nothing is uploaded.
• Open source — anyone can inspect the code.

WHO IT'S FOR
• Villages and interior areas with weak 2G/3G signal.
• Old or budget phones where big payment apps are slow or unsupported.
• Anywhere data drops but calls still work — basements, trains, events.

OruBar Pay is a QR scanner and *99# dialing helper. It does not hold or move money.
Payments run on NPCI's public *99# service from your own bank-registered SIM.

Languages: Tamil, English, Kannada, Hindi.
Support: orubar@arivanandhan.in
```
